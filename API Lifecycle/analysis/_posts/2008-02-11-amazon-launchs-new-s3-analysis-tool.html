--- layout: post title: 'Amazon Launchs new S3 Analysis Tool' source: http://analysis.apievangelist.com/2008/02/11/amazon-launchs-new-s3-analysis-tool/ domain: analysis.apievangelist.com image: atomdate: 2008-02-11T22:39:00-08:00 --- Amazon just launched a new analysis tool for Amazon S3 called, S3Stat. This tool uses the log files generated by S3, analyzes them using Webalizer, and generates a variety of reports. Take a look at the sample reports to learn more. There's a one-month free trial and usage after that costs just $2 per month. Take a look at the pricing plan to learn more. While you are on the site you may want to take a look at their list of S3 resources as well.
